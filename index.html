Kelly O's

I Love This Bar!

Kelly O'Brian's is a popular Irish pub located in Colorado Springs, known for its lively atmosphere and friendly service. Offering a cozy yet vibrant setting, it features classic Irish pub fare, a great selection of beers, and a variety of cocktails. With its inviting ambiance, live music, and sports screenings, Kelly O'Brian's is a favorite spot for both locals and visitors looking to unwind, enjoy good food, and experience a genuine Irish pub vibe.
